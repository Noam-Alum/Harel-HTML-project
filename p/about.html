About Us

Oracle Corporation is a multinational computer technology company that offers software, cloud solutions, and hardware products. Founded in 1977, Oracle has transformed the way organizations manage their data and deliver information. Our mission is to help businesses of all sizes harness the power of data to drive innovation and growth.

"Innovation distinguishes between a leader and a follower." – Steve Jobs

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower businesses with cutting-edge technology that enhances their capabilities, drives efficiency, and enables them to make informed decisions based on data.

Core Values

  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of honesty and transparency.
  • Customer Success: Our customers are at the heart of everything we do.
  • Innovation: We continually seek out new ideas and approaches to solve complex challenges.
  • Collaboration: We believe in the power of teamwork to achieve our goals.

Our History

Founded by Larry Ellison, Bob Miner, and Ed Oates in 1977, Oracle started as a company focused on database software. Over the years, we have expanded our offerings to include cloud applications, hardware systems, and enterprise solutions, becoming a leader in the tech industry.

Oracle provides a wide range of products and services designed to help businesses thrive in a digital world:

  • Database Solutions: Industry-leading database technology for managing large volumes of data.
  • Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP): Integrated applications for financial management, project management, and more.
  • Customer Experience (CX): Tools and solutions to enhance customer engagement and satisfaction.
